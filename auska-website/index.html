The protocol · Surface to wallet

Resource in. $AUSKA out.

Underneath the demo is a plain three-step loop. No wrapped-wrapper tokens, no yield borrowed from a yield borrowed from a yield. Three stages, all onchain, all readable by anyone.

01

Stake a claim

Every claim is a unique onchain slot tied to a real extractive market — WTI, BRENT, NATGAS, COPPER, URANIUM, and the rest of the board. Supply per commodity is fixed. Early claimants get the richest layers; latecomers work the tailings.

02

Break the layer

Claims produce while you work them. Output tracks the real volatility of the underlying commodity — when the market rips or dumps, matching claims run hot and throughput multiplies. A quiet market means a slow trickle. A supply shock means every claim on the board lights up.

03

Refine to $AUSKA

Extracted resource settles to $AUSKA at the surface. Sell it, hold it, or burn it to deepen your claim and raise its base output — the only sink in the system, and it's a shovel. The protocol fee recipient takes a cut of every settlement to fund the reserves board.
